Intro

Once upon a time, there lived a not so skinny raccoon named Scippie. Now, Scippie had refined tastes: grapes, obviously, but also the finer things in life. We’re talking Renaissance paintings, dramatic architecture, and the occasional swirl of classical music. A raccoon of culture.

Naturally, this meant he absolutely adored Rosalía. To Scippie, her LUX album wasn’t just good. It was liquid gold poured straight into his tiny, glittering eyes. And tonight? Tonight was the night. He was finally going to see her live.

There was just one small problem: his (favorite) dad. We’ll call him Quinsys. Quinsys, while well-meaning, was not exactly… artistically enlightened. And that simply wouldn’t do. Because if Scippie could successfully educate Quinsys -truly open his mind to the artistic brilliance he was about to witness- then Scippie’s second-favorite dad (a competitive title), Quinten, would undoubtedly reward Quinsys with a generous supply of alcoholic beverages.

And here’s the genius part: Quinsys becomes very agreeable when tipsy. Agreeable Quinsys = more grapes for Scippie.

Everyone wins. Especially Scippie.

So the mission is clear: help our cultured little bandit track down as many artistic references featured in the LUX tour as possible. He’ll curate his very own gallery, go visit (and tap) him, and armed with this knowledge, you’ll be fully prepared to dominate the concert bingo during the show tonight, earning drinks, glory, and possibly eternal raccoon respect.
